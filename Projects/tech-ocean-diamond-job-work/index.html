Tech Ocean – Diamond Job Work Management

A desktop-based ERP solution designed specifically for the diamond industry to manage job work, inventory movement, salary calculation, billing, and financial records with accuracy and control.

What This Software Solves

  • Accurate Job In / Job Out tracking using Jangad numbers
  • Precise handling of pieces (Pic) and carat weight (Ct)
  • Automated salary calculation based on actual work output
  • Clear visibility of stock movement and pending jobs
  • Professional billing, invoices, and reports

Key Features

Job Work Management

Manage job issue, job return, and work stages with full traceability.

Salary & Accounts

Calculate salaries from work output and maintain income & expense records.

Billing & Reports

Generate GST-ready bills, salary slips, and operational reports in PDF.

Import & Export

Handle high-volume data using structured CSV import and export utilities.

Who This Is For

  • Diamond job work units
  • Small to mid-size diamond manufacturers
  • Factory supervisors and accounting teams

Beta Status

This product is currently in limited beta. Features and workflows may evolve based on feedback from real industry users. The beta phase focuses on correctness, usability, and operational validation.
